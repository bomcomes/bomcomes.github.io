02. Purpose of Collection and Use of Personal Information

"Personal information" refers to information about a living individual that makes it possible to identify the individual through name, resident registration number, images, etc. (including information that can be easily combined with other information to identify a specific individual) in accordance with Article 2, Paragraph 1 of the Personal Information Protection Act. The Company does not disclose users' personal information without prior consent and uses the collected information as follows :

  • ① Confirmation of intent to join, user identification, confirmation of intent to withdraw, and other member management activities.
  • ② Delivery of promised services, identity verification for service provision, handling and replying to inquiries or complaints, and sending notices.
  • ③ Restrictions against members who violate laws and terms of use, prevention and sanctions against acts that interfere with the smooth operation of the service including unauthorized use, account theft and fraudulent transaction prevention, preservation of records for dispute resolution, and other user protection and service operation.
  • ④ New service development and customized service provision, service provision and advertising based on demographic characteristics, analysis of service usage records, and other service improvement.
  • ⑤ Providing event information and participation opportunities, providing advertising information, and other marketing and promotion (Only for information subjects who have consented. You may refuse consent; however, please be informed that you may not receive information about new services or events.)

※ When using the community service, information generated from activities such as writing posts and comments is used for providing community functions, communication between users, and operational management.
